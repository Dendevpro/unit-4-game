Crystal Collector

Are You Ready to Collect Some Crystals?

INSTRUCTIONS:
  • You will be given a TARGET number at the start of every new game.

  • Click on the crystals to add points to your Score.

  • You will not know the value of each crystal until you click on it.

  • Win the game by matching your Score to the Target number.

  • If you go over, you will lose.

  • Important: Everytime the game restarts, the crystals values change.
